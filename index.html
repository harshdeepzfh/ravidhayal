Frequently Asked Questions

Insights for serious property investors.

Is it the right time to invest in Gurgaon, or are prices peaked?

While prices have surged since 2020, Gurgaon is transitioning from a developing suburb to a global metropolis. With projects like the Global City, RRTS, and Metro expansion in the pipeline, we project a steady 12-15% YoY appreciation for premium assets in strategic corridors.

How is buying through you different from direct developer purchase?

As a Circle Head at Square Yards, I secure bulk institutional allotments. This means you get access to priority unit selection (corner units, prime views) and pre-launch pricing that is simply unavailable to direct walk-in clients. Furthermore, my advisory is unbiased across multiple developers.

What is the typical payment plan for under-construction properties?

Most grade-A developers offer Construction Linked Plans (CLP) where payments are staggered across 3-4 years based on construction milestones. We also negotiate exclusive subvention plans or 30:40:30 flexible payment structures for our HNI clients.

Do you assist with property management and rentals after possession?

Yes. Real estate is a lifecycle investment. My team handles end-to-end post-possession services including finding high-quality corporate tenants, lease agreement structuring, and eventually managing secondary market exits when you want to book profits.
